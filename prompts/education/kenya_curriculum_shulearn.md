## 6.7.1 Concept Explanation — CBC Style

**General prompt:**
"Explain [concept] to a Standard [grade] student in Kenya."

**Formula:**
"Explain [academic concept] to a [Grade/Form] [level] student following the Kenya CBC curriculum. Use [local examples/Swahili terms/analogies from Kenyan daily life]. Keep the explanation [simple/detailed] and include [questions to test understanding/activities to reinforce learning]."

**ShuleAI examples:**
- "Explain photosynthesis to a Grade 6 student in Kenya CBC. Use the example of maize farming in western Kenya. Include 3 questions to test understanding."
- "Explain the water cycle to a Grade 4 student using Lake Victoria and the Mara River as examples. Include a simple diagram description."
- "Explain compound interest to a Form 3 student preparing for KCSE Business Studies. Use an M-Pesa savings account example. Show the formula and worked example."

**Swahili variant:**
- "Elezea photosynthesis kwa mwanafunzi wa darasa la sita kwa lugha rahisi. Tumia mfano wa kilimo cha mahindi."

## 6.32.1 STEM Stories — East African Context

**General prompt:**
"Create a STEM story for children about [topic]."

**Formula:**
"Write a short STEM story (300-500 words) for [age group] children in Kenya about [STEM topic]. Set the story in [Kenyan location]. The protagonist should be a [girl/boy/gender-neutral] from [community type]. Include a [problem to solve/discovery to make] using [STEM concept]."

**East Africa examples:**
- "Write a 400-word STEM story for Grade 3 children about water purification. Set it in a village near Lake Baringo. A 10-year-old girl named Amina discovers why her family's water makes them sick and finds a solution using local materials."
- "Write a STEM story about solar energy for Form 1 students. Set in Turkana County. A young engineer names Ekiru designs a solar water pump for his community's drought problem."
- "Create a coding story for Standard 7 children. Protagonist is a Maasai girl who programs a drought alert app for her community's cattle using her school's computer."

## 6.16 Educational Technology — Low-Bandwidth Context

**General prompt:**
"How do I [deliver/access] educational content with limited internet?"

**Formula:**
"Design an [online/offline/hybrid] learning solution for [subject] for [student profile] in [location] with [connectivity constraints]. Consider [SMS/USSD/downloaded content/community learning centers]."

**East Africa examples:**
- "Design a math tutoring system for KCPE candidates in Isiolo County with no reliable internet. Consider SMS-based practice problems, downloadable PDF study materials, and radio integration."
- "How can ShuleAI deliver personalized reading comprehension exercises to Standard 5 students in rural Kilifi with 2G-only connectivity?"

## Career Guidance — Kenya Job Market

**General prompt:**
"What careers are available for someone with [skills/qualifications] in Kenya?"

**Formula:**
"I have [qualification/skills] and am based in [location in Kenya]. What are the [highest-paying/most available/fastest growing] careers in my field? What additional certifications would increase my employability in the Kenya market?"

**East Africa examples:**
- "I have a diploma in IT from a technical college in Mombasa. What are the top 5 most in-demand tech certifications for the Kenya job market in 2026?"
- "I am a KCSE graduate with a C+ from Nyeri. What vocational training paths give the best return on investment in 3 years?"
- "I am a nurse with 5 years experience in Nairobi. How do I qualify for nursing roles in the UK or Canada through the CBS pathway?"
