# Health Prompts — AfyaAI, Kenya Health System

> Adapted from 1337 Use Cases for ChatGPT (Section 9: Health and Medicine)
> East African context: Kenya health system (Level 4/5 hospitals, CHVs), NHIF/SHA, maternal health, tropical disease

---

## Patient Education — Kenya Context

**General prompt:**
"Explain [medical condition] to a patient in plain language."

**Formula:**
"Explain [medical condition/procedure/medication] to a patient in Kenya in plain English (or Swahili). Avoid medical jargon. Include: what it is, causes, symptoms, when to seek care at [dispensary/health centre/Level 4 hospital], and home management advice."

**AfyaAI examples:**
- "Explain gestational diabetes to a 28-year-old pregnant woman in rural Kenya with Standard 8 education. Include dietary advice relevant to local foods (ugali, sukuma wiki, githeri). Mention warning signs that require hospital visit."
- "Explain how to use an ORS (oral rehydration salts) sachet to a parent whose child has diarrhea. Include locally available alternatives if ORS is not accessible."
- "Explain NHIF/SHA registration and benefits to an informal sector worker in Nairobi. What's covered, what's not, and which Level 4 hospitals they can access."

## Maternal Health — Third Trimester

**General prompt:**
"What are the warning signs during [pregnancy stage] that require immediate care?"

**Formula:**
"What are the warning signs during [pregnancy week/trimester] that a pregnant woman in Kenya should watch for? For each warning sign, specify: (1) the risk it indicates, (2) whether to go to dispensary/health centre/Level 5 hospital, (3) what to say to the healthcare provider."

**East Africa examples:**
- "What are warning signs at 30 weeks pregnancy that require immediate hospital care? List each sign, what it could indicate, and whether to go to a Level 4 or Level 5 facility. Format as a simple checklist."
- "Explain pre-eclampsia symptoms to a 32-week pregnant woman. What blood pressure readings require emergency care? Which Level 5 hospitals in Nairobi have obstetric ICU?"
- "Create a birth preparedness plan for a rural woman in Kisii County with no reliable transport. Include: when to start the journey, what to bring, where to go, emergency contacts."

## Nutrition for Pregnancy — East African Foods

**General prompt:**
"What should I eat during [pregnancy stage] for optimal nutrition?"

**Formula:**
"Create a weekly meal plan for a [trimester] pregnant woman in Kenya on a budget of KES [amount]. Use locally available East African foods. Ensure adequate [iron/calcium/protein/folate/DHA]. Avoid [listeria risks/high mercury fish/unpasteurized dairy]."

**East Africa examples:**
- "Create a daily meal plan for a 30-week pregnant woman in Nairobi on KES 300/day. Use locally available foods: sukuma wiki, eggs, beans, omena, ugali, sweet potatoes. Ensure 27mg iron, 1000mg calcium, 71g protein per day."
- "Which East African foods are highest in iron for a pregnant woman? Rank by iron content per serving and bioavailability. Include both plant and animal sources."
- "List East African fish that are safe to eat during pregnancy. Which Kenyan lake fish are high in DHA and low in mercury?"
