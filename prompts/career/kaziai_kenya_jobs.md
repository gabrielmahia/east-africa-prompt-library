## Kenya Job Market Analysis

**General prompt:**
"What are the highest-demand jobs in Kenya in [year]?"

**Formula:**
"List the [number] highest-demand [sector] jobs in Kenya as of [year]. For each: salary range in KES, required qualifications, top employers, growth trajectory, and how someone from [background] can transition into this role in [timeframe]."

**KaziAI examples:**
- "List the 10 highest-demand tech jobs in Kenya in 2026. For each: salary range, required skills, top employers (local and international), and how a software engineering graduate from University of Nairobi can land this role within 6 months."
- "I am a Form 4 leaver with a C plain from Kisumu. What vocational training paths give the best income within 2 years? Compare: plumbing, electrical, welding, and motorcycle mechanics."
- "I am a nurse with 5 years experience in Kenyatta National Hospital. What international nursing pathways are open to me: UK CBT, Canadian NCLEX, US NCLEX-RN? Compare cost, timeline, and likelihood of success."

## CV and Interview Preparation — Kenya Context

**General prompt:**
"Help me write a CV for [job] in Kenya."

**Formula:**
"Write a professional CV for someone applying for [job title] at [company type] in Kenya. The candidate has: [education], [years] years of experience in [field], skills in [skills list]. Format it for Kenyan employers. Include a cover letter opening paragraph addressing [specific requirement]."

**East Africa examples:**
- "Write a CV for a data analyst position at Equity Bank Kenya. Candidate has: BSc Statistics from Egerton University, 3 years at KenGen in data reporting, skills in Excel, Power BI, Python, and SQL."
- "Write an interview preparation guide for a systems administrator role at Safaricom. Include: likely technical questions, Kenyan workplace culture expectations, salary negotiation approach, and questions to ask the interviewer."

## International Job Applications — From Kenya

**General prompt:**
"How do I apply for [job] abroad from Kenya?"

**Formula:**
"Guide a Kenyan [profession] with [qualifications] and [years] years experience through applying for [job type] in [destination country]. Cover: credential recognition, required exams/certifications, typical visa pathway, salary expectations, and realistic timeline from application to employment."

**East Africa examples:**
- "How does a Kenyan architect with 8 years experience apply for jobs in the UAE? Cover: ESMA architectural license recognition, typical salary ranges in Dubai, work visa pathway, and recommended recruitment agencies."
- "I am a Kenyan ICT teacher wanting to teach in the UK. Guide me through: UK QTS recognition pathway, DBS check process, visa (Skilled Worker route), and realistic timeline."
- "A Kenyan doctor wants to work in Canada. Explain the MCCQE1 and MCCQE2 pathway, provincial licensing, IMM requirements, and realistic timeline from starting exam prep to first posting."
