# Contributing to East Africa Prompt Library

Thank you for your interest. This is a focused resource — quality over quantity.

## What we accept

- Prompts that are **specific to East African contexts** — Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Ethiopia, or the wider Horn of Africa
- Prompts that follow the **General → Formula → Specific** pattern
- Prompts that work with [mpesa-mcp](https://github.com/gabrielmahia/mpesa-mcp), [wapimaji-mcp](https://github.com/gabrielmahia/wapimaji-mcp), or other tools in the East African Decision Infrastructure portfolio
- Swahili or multilingual variants of existing prompts
- Domain-specific additions: diaspora, agriculture, informal sector, devolution/county government

## What we do not accept

- Generic prompts not adapted for East Africa
- Prompts copied verbatim from other resources without adaptation
- Anything requiring proprietary data not available in the region

## How to contribute

**File an issue** describing the prompt category and at least two specific examples.

No unsolicited PRs. All contributions reviewed by Gabriel Mahia before merging.

## Prompt format

Every prompt must follow this structure:

```markdown
## [Use Case Name]

**General prompt:**
"[Plain language example]"

**Formula:**
"[Template with [placeholders]]"

**East Africa examples:**
- "[Kenya/region-specific example]"
- "[Swahili or multilingual variant]"
- "[Low-bandwidth/rural context variant if applicable]"
```
